In this guide, we'll describe what you can do on each page of the Blox-Grow dashboard. This will help you get familiar with the platform and make the most out of your experience.

## Dashboard Overview
When you log in to your Blox-Grow account, you'll be greeted by the dashboard.

The [dashboard page](https://beta.bloxgrow.best/dashboard) provides an overview of your account and news about the community. Here's what you can do on this page:
- **View your account statistics**: This includes your current balance, total earnings, total tasks completed, and recent activity.
- **Check out the leaderboard**: See how you rank against other users in terms of current balance.
- **View website news**: Stay updated with the latest events, updates, and announcements from Blox-Grow.

## Earning Page Overview
The [earning page](https://beta.bloxgrow.best/earn) is where you can find various tasks to complete in exchange for Robux. Here's what you can do on this page:
- **Browse available tasks**: View a list of tasks, including surveys, videos, and app downloads.
- **Filter tasks**: Use filters to find tasks that suit your interests or preferences.
- **Start tasks**: Click on a task to begin earning Robux. Follow the instructions provided for each task to ensure successful completion.

## Withdrawal Page Overview
The [withdrawal page](https://beta.bloxgrow.best/withdraw) is where you can request Robux withdrawals or payouts. Here's what you can do on this page:
- **Request a withdrawal**: Choose the amount of Robux you want to withdraw and select your preferred withdrawal method (e.g., group payout, game pass).
- **View withdrawal history**: Check your past withdrawal requests and their statuses.
- **Check withdrawal limits**: Be aware of any minimum or maximum withdrawal limits that may apply.

## Settings Page Overview
The [settings page](https://beta.bloxgrow.best/settings) allows you to customize your Blox-Grow account. Here's what you can do:
- **Update your profile**: Change your username, email address, and password.
- **Connect social accounts**: Link your Discord or other social accounts for easier access and security.
