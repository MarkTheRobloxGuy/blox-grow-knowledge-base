# Create an Account

Click the **Start Earning** button in the top-middle of the [website](https://bloxgrow.best) or go directly to [the sign up page](https://beta.bloxgrow.best/signup).

You can sign up using:
- Your **email address**
- Or a secure **social login** (Discord, Google etc.)

Do not use your Roblox credentials here; we never ask for your Roblox password.
You must create a custom username and password for Blox-Grow.

### Why Sign Up?

- **Free to use** — no hidden fees, no hidden withdrawal limits/minimums.
- **Fast payouts** with Roblox gamepasses, Roblox group payouts, and Paypal.

### Is Blox-Grow Safe?

Absolutely! We use secure connections and never ask for your Roblox login. All Robux are sent through Roblox built-in methods like **group payouts or game passes**.
